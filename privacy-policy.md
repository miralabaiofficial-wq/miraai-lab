# Privacy Policy
Last Updated: March 2026

Welcome to MiraAI Lab Automation Tool ("the App", "we", "our", "us").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when using our application in connection with TikTok's API services.

---

## 1. Overview

MiraAI Lab Automation Tool is an application designed to assist content creators in generating and publishing AI-created short-form videos to TikTok.

We are committed to protecting user privacy and complying with TikTok Platform Policies and Developer Terms.

---

## 2. Information We Access

When you connect your TikTok account to this application, we may access:

- Basic TikTok account authentication data (OAuth tokens)
- Account identifier necessary for publishing content
- Content publishing permissions granted by the user

We do NOT access:
- Passwords
- Private messages
- Followers' personal data
- Sensitive personal information

---

## 3. How We Use Information

The information accessed is used solely for:

- Authenticating your TikTok account
- Publishing videos on your behalf
- Managing automated content posting features

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or resale purposes.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

Authentication tokens may be stored securely for the purpose of enabling automated posting functionality.

We do not permanently store personal data.

Users may request deletion of stored authentication data at any time by contacting us.

Upon request, all stored tokens and related data will be deleted within a reasonable timeframe.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

- Sell user data
- Share user data with third parties
- Transfer user data for marketing purposes

Data is only used internally for application functionality.

---

## 6. User Responsibility

Users are solely responsible for the content they create and publish using this application.

We are not responsible for content violations, account suspensions, or actions taken by TikTok due to user-generated content.

---

## 7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect authentication data and prevent unauthorized access.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

---

## 8. Compliance with TikTok Policies

This application is designed to comply with:

- TikTok Platform Policies
- TikTok Developer Terms of Service

We only access data necessary for the functionality explicitly approved by the user.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for individuals under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction).

We do not knowingly collect data from children.
